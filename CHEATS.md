## Dev Tools

Type `DEVMOD` to open an interactive panel that lets you tweak base stats in real time:

- **Player:** Speed, Max HP, Damage, Radius, Invulnerability time
- **Melee:** Attack range, cooldown, duration, knockback
- **Dagger:** Cooldown, damage multiplier, speed, range
- **Dash:** Speed multiplier, duration, cooldown
- **Enemy:** Speed, HP, Damage, Radius, XP per kill
- **Projectile:** Speed, damage
- **Misc:** Drop chance, training respawn delay
